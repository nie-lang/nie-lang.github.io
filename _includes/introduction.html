Lang Nie   聂浪

I am currently a Wenfeng Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science, Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications (CQUPT). Prior to that, I received my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU) in 2019 and 2025, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Chunyu Lin and Prof. Yao Zhao. I have also been fortunate to work with Prof. Shuaicheng Liu at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC).

My research interests cover a range of computer vision and deep learning. I have authored or co-authored about 20 academic papers in top-tier international conferences and journals, including CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, TPAMI, TIP, TMM, TCSVT, etc. I'm open to any kind of collaboration. Please feel free to contact me through email.
